/ DESIGN ENGINEER

I design the experiences that I build.

Hey, I'm Damilare Okanlawon—often known as Drey. I am a visual designer who also writes clean, functional code.

My process covers the entire creative pipeline. I use CorelDRAW to craft solid brand identities, logos, and marketing flyers. I then move into Figma to wireframe and prototype intuitive UI/UX website designs. Finally, I bring those exact layouts to life in the browser using HTML5, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript. Because I am the one designing the graphics and writing the code, nothing gets lost in translation. I ensure every vector asset, layout grid, and responsive element scales perfectly onto any screen size.
